**Clip any web page or selection into clean Markdown — edit it, then send it to your AI.**

Open the side panel next to any page. Capture the whole page or just your selection
as clean, readable Markdown, edit it right there, then copy it or send it to the AI
assistant of your choice. No accounts, no API keys — everything is assembled on your
machine.

## What it does

- **Capture** a page or selection as fenced Markdown, dropped at your cursor.
- **Clip** a selection as an attributed blockquote; **extract tables**; **pull page metadata**.
- **Edit** in a plain-Markdown editor that persists across restarts.
- **Send to an AI** — Claude, ChatGPT, or Gemini — by copy-and-open. You paste it; nothing is auto-submitted.
- **Workflows:** save and re-run your own multi-step capture + send sequences in one click.
- **Privacy by design:** no servers, no analytics, nothing leaves your machine unless you copy or export it.

## Install

**[Download v0.5.1](https://github.com/saarsg/mark-clipper/releases/latest/download/mark-clipper-v0.5.1.zip)**,
unzip, then load the folder via `chrome://extensions` → Developer mode → Load unpacked.
